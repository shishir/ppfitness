About Pro Performance Fitness

Pro performance is a high performance training facility designed for athletes and those looking to make a serious commitment to their career. Pro performance provides athletes with an environment in which they can identify and work towards quantifiable improvements both inside and outside the training facility.

  • SPORTS CONDITIONING

    Strength, speed, power, agility& co-ordination, aerobic conditioning and flexibility training

  • STRONGMAN TRAINING

    High intensity training with truck tires, kettle bells, fitness ropes, hammer, iron bars, hardcore boxing, parachutes, medicine & Swiss balls, resistance bands.

  • FITNESS FOR KIDS

    innovative programming provides kids with the opportunity to build a healthy foundation for life.Skill development through variety of ball drills, ladder & cone drills and kids yoga.

Why Choose Us

Our mission is to provide and maintain high quality fitness programs that will sustain the growth and development of Athletes through comprehensive assessments, programming, rehabilitation and all aspects of fitness training.

Certified Trainers

Our trainers have formal education in Fitness from reputed Institutions in India and overseas.

Not Regular Gym

We are dedicated to develop young Indian Athletes of all age groups and help develop positive character traits.

Personal Oversight

You will always have our trainers working with you. No subscribing to gym and paying for Personal Trainers.

We are Fun

We have a variety of functional, yoga, ladders, ropes, agility, boxing which makes workouts exciting. We refuse to keep our workouts boring.

Outdoor Training

We have regular outdoor training sessions which you can enjoy. When was the last time you ran up the slope of a hill?

Made to Fit

Periodized plans for everyone. A marathoner trains different from a boxer :) and we understand the difference.
